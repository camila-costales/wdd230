Carlsbad State Beach (Tamarack Beach)

Also known simply as Tamarack Beach, Carlsbad State Beach is one of the most popular and easily accessible beaches in Carlsbad. It stretches for several miles along the Pacific Coast Highway (Highway 101) and offers a wide expanse of sandy shoreline, making it a great destination for families, beachgoers, and surfers alike.

South Carlsbad State Beach (Ponto Beach)

South Carlsbad State Beach, also referred to as Ponto Beach, is located just south of Carlsbad State Beach and offers a more relaxed and serene atmosphere. It's favored by locals and visitors alike, and its long sandy shoreline provides ample space for various beach activities. One of the unique features of South Carlsbad State Beach is its campground, where visitors can enjoy camping just steps away from the ocean.
