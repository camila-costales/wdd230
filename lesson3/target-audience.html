Target Audience

My Target Audience include local businesses, residents, and visitors. The website would serve as a valuable resource for businesses, providing information and resources on how to start, grow, and market their business, and connecting them with potential partners or customers. For residents, the website would be a go-to source for information on local events, news, and community services, and a platform for engagement with their community. Visitors to Quito would also benefit from the website, which could provide them with information on local attractions, events, and businesses, helping them plan their trip and make the most of their time in the city.
